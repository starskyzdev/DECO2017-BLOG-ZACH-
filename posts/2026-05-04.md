Based on our research, which emphasises the importance of a "community-driven baking lab," we created low-fidelity wireframes. Our goal was to design an interface that facilitates process review, troubleshooting, and structured critique.

The wireframes are deliberately simple, focusing on the hierarchy of information and workflow rather than visual elements.

## The Community Feed: Designed for Scanning

![My diagram](assets/bakeFeed.png)

The homepage serves as our community feed, showcasing structured bake cards in a desktop-first, two-column grid format. Unlike Instagram or Facebook, which promote passive scrolling, our feed is designed for quick scanning and assessment.

Each card highlights key variables right away,feedback type they want, outcome, and the specific question the baker is posing. The sidebar filters allow users to sort by hydration level or feedback type, making it easy to find bakes they are qualified to critique.

## The Standout Feature: The Diagnosis Page

![My diagram](assets/bakeDetail.png)

The Bake Detail page is the most important screen in our app. We intentionally framed this not as a "post," but as a diagnostic workspace.

It uses a single-column reading flow that guides the user through the diagnostic process from top to bottom:
1. **Outcome:** The photo, title, and result summary.
2. **Key Bake Data:** A structured grid of variables (hydration, temperatures, fold counts, times).
3. **Process Notes:** The baker's qualitative observations.
4. **Community Critique:** A structured form for visitors to leave feedback.
5. **Comments:** The ongoing discussion.

This layout reduces cognitive overload. Visitors have all the context they need before they even reach the comment box.

## Structuring the Critique

To solve the research finding that "online advice lacks context," we structured the feedback mechanism itself. When leaving a comment, users must select a **Feedback Type** (e.g., Crumb, Oven Spring, Fermentation) and a **Confidence Level** (Just a Guess, Pretty Sure, Experienced with This). 

This forces honesty and makes the advice far more useful to the original baker.

## Logging Everything

To ensure no detail is missed, the bake log provides comprehensive input so that everything is well recorded

This provides better context and clarity for community to provide feedback and help.

![My diagram](assets/bakeLog.png)

## Habit Forming: The Starter Log

![My diagram](assets/starterLog.png)

To drive the daily retention the brief asked for, we included a lightweight Starter Log. This allows users to quickly track their feeding times, rise percentages, and smell notes. It is fast to fill in and becomes highly valuable over time, giving users a reason to return even on days they are not actively baking.
